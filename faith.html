For Faith Communities

Your care ministry, coordinated.

Give your congregation a rotating pool of accounts to activate for families walking through a new baby, an illness, a loss, or a deployment — without the spreadsheet and the group text.

The walkdown

From pool to a family cared for

1
A ministry leader sets up the care pool
Your church gets a block of accounts to activate as needs arise.
2
Provision a family in need
Add their name, contact, reason for care, and how long you expect to support them — that window sets the account.
3
The family gets their own kitchen
A scoped view with just their meals and calendar — no billing, no settings, nothing to manage.
4
Days remaining keeps the pool rotating
When a family’s season ends, the slot returns to the pool for the next family.
Features

What you get

Account pool
A fixed set of accounts you activate and recycle across families all year.
Scoped recipient view
Families see only their kitchen — never billing or other families.
Days remaining gating
Each account runs for the window you set, then returns to the pool.
Meal history
Coordinators see what’s been sent so meals never repeat.
Text confirmation
No meal is charged until the family confirms by text.
Data export
Pull your roster and giving records for the board anytime.
Access & roles
Ministry Admin
Sets up the pool, provisions families, sees every account and the giving history.
Recipient (family)
A stripped down kitchen — their restaurants, calendar, and address only. No billing, no other accounts, nothing to delete.
