Most food benefits fail on utilization. YourKitchen drives activation through a member’s own village — and gives you the clinical protocols, surveys, and reporting to prove it.

The walkdown

From enrollment to a measurable outcome

1
Enroll members at scale
A filterable console for tens of thousands of members, with search, facets, and bulk operations.
2
Assign a clinical nutrition protocol
Gate the menu to condition appropriate meals — low sodium, low glycemic, renal — per member.
3
Members activate through their village
Meals from the people who love them get used — utilization is the whole game.
4
Soft-touch surveys after every delivery
Ease, mood, food quality, delivery, helpfulness — one-tap PROs for CAHPS, HEDIS, and Star narratives.
5
Report and model ROI
Activation, on time, satisfaction, cost per member, and an ROI you can adjust against your population.
Features

Full access — enrollment, protocols, reporting, and settings.
